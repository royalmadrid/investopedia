Automated Investing 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Automated investing is the use of digital platforms to make pre-programmed investing and trading decisions for customers based on algorithms and variables from the user such as age, income, goals, and risk tolerance.

Blockchain Technology
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Blockchain creates a faster, more efficient way for businesses to transmit, receive, and track orders using secure data. It is the foundation of cryptocurrency trading and is moving into regulated industries such as banking and auctions.
